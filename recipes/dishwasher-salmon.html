Dishwasher Salmon Recipe

Description

Yes, this is a real recipe.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Lemon
  • Sprig fresh rosemary
  • Kosher salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Wrap the filet, 1/2 lemon, and rosemary tightly in tin foil.
  2. Place foil packet in the top rack of your dishwasher.
  3. Use the heated water and heated dry cycle.
  4. Salt and Pepper to taste- plate and serve immediately.
  5. Clap.
